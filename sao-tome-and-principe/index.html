Cryptocurrencies themselves, like tokens / coins, have a price that people charge them when they make transactions on exchanges. And when it comes to the cryptocurrency market, they are especially different. Many currencies are grossly overvalued while others are grossly undervalued.

Cryptocurrencies are not just tokens. These are innovative projects based on blockchain. And it is some projects that can greatly affect the development of various sectors of Sao Tome and Principe`s economy and make life easier for people. Such projects have real value. Often, coins are just an addition to them or a small element that is part of a certain complex system.
